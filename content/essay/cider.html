--- title: "Cider" date: 2018-01-08T22:39:57Z draft: true author: "Glen Campbell" category: essays tags: [cider,drink,alcohol,apple] --- In the last few months, I've taken a liking to cider. This is not the apple juice drink that you might have grown up with, but "hard cider," usually 5-7% alcohol by volume (ABV). For me, it's much more enjoyable than the vast majority of beers. (I'll be honest; there are very few beers that I really enjoy, so I tend to stay away from them.)
